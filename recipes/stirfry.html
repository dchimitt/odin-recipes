Chicken Broccoli Stirfry

Recipe taken from Eat Well 101

Description:

This is a recipe for making chicken and broccoli stirfry.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless/skinless chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 medium carrot, shredded
  • 8 ounces of noodles of choice
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • Fresh scallion for garnish
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Premade sauce mix

Steps:

  1. Boil a large pot of water, salted, and cook noodles according to package. Add broccoli florets during last 5 minutes of noodles cooking. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season chicken cubes to taste with salt and pepper. Add olive oil to skillet and cook chicken over medium heat, turning every 30 seconds for 2-3 minutes until slightly golden.
  3. Stir in garlic and carrots, cook for another minute.
  4. Transfer cooked noodles and broccoli to skillet. Pour sauce on top and toss until incorporated.
  5. Reheat all ingredients for 3 minutes, then garnish with scallion and sesame seeds.
  6. Ready to serve!
